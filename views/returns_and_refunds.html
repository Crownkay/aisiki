Returns and Refunds

What is this Privacy Policy

Aisiki is a Business-to-Business company and operates with perishables, and hence does not support returns after delivery has been successfully made and recorded. However, customers reserve the rights to reject or refuse to accept our product only at the actual point and time of delivery. If for any valid reasons, after appropriate investigation has been carried out within 1-3 business days, and our product is deemed fit to be rejected or not to be accepted by the concerned customer, and such customer has already made payments for the said order before delivery, Aisiki would refund such customer within 1-10 business days after the official date of release of the investigation report. Such refund shall be based on the recommendation(s) contained in the said report. In case you have any queries related to returns and refunds, please write to support@aisiki.co
